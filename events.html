Events

Paws for a Cuppa hosts poetry nights, educational events, adoption events, and many other activities! If you'd like to suggest an event, please email us at manager@pawscafe.com.

You can find our event calendar on our Facebook here. In order to sign up for our events, please:

  1. Sign up or log in to your Facebook account
  2. Click the link above or search Paws for a Cuppa in the Facebook search bar.
  3. Click on the "Upcoming Events" button.
  4. Click "Going" on the event you would like to attend. (Please remember to check the event description -- some events have an entrance fee!)
